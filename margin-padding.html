Margin and Padding in Bootstrap

Margin Classes

m-3 (margin all sides)
mt-4 (top margin)
mb-4 (bottom margin)
ms-4 (start/left margin)
me-4 (end/right margin)
mx-5 (horizontal margin)
my-5 (vertical margin)

Padding Classes

p-3 (padding all sides)
pt-4 (top padding)
pb-4 (bottom padding)
ps-4 (start/left padding)
pe-4 (end/right padding)
px-5 (horizontal padding)
py-5 (vertical padding)
